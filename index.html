    Welcome To

    Lucky Nails Salon

    At Lucky Nails, we pride ourselves on creating a relaxed, calm, and friendly atmosphere, while delivering a high standard of professionalism and hygiene. You’ll experience truly unique nail art and you’re sure to leave the salon luxurious and feeling rejuvenated.

    We vow to have you feeling pampered from the moment you enter, with endless supplies of colors and a great relaxing environment for you to unwind.

    Lucky Nails is conveniently located at Brattleboro, Vermont, and is making it easily accessible for our guests. It’s the perfect place to escape the stresses of life and relax while you’re pampered by a friendly technician, in a pleasant environment.

    Caring about the needs of our clients is what has been and what will continue to be our main focus.

    Services for Every Occasion

    Discover our premium nail and beauty services designed to make you look and feel your best!

    Manicure

    Professional nail shaping, cuticle care, and polish application with a relaxing hand massage and moisturizing treatment.

    Pedicure

    Complete foot care with nail trimming, callus removal, exfoliating scrub, and soothing massage. Finished with your choice of polish.

    Full Set / Fill

    Custom acrylic or gel extensions for desired length and shape. Full sets create new nails, fills maintain existing sets with fresh polish.

    Professional hair removal for eyebrows, face, legs, and arms using high-quality wax for smooth, long-lasting results.
